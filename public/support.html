üéÆ Getting Started

How do I play INCEPT≈∏ON?

INCEPT≈∏ON is an educational idle/clicker game. Here's how to start:

  1. Tap the habitat (Europa's ocean) to generate Research Points (RP)
  2. Unlock species in the Species Lab (first species costs 30 RP, unlocks in ~30 seconds)
  3. Species generate Biomass (BM) passively‚Äîeven when offline!
  4. Purchase upgrades in the Engineering tab to boost production
  5. Survive events (Gravity Glitch, Alien Hype Cycle, etc.) for bonus resources
  6. Unlock fact cards in the Encyclopedia to learn real planetary science
  7. Synthesize Europa when ready (prestige) to earn Galactic Reputation and permanent upgrades

What are the resources?

  • RP (Research Points): Earned by tapping. Used to unlock species and upgrades.
  • BM (Biomass): Generated by species. Used for upgrades and prestige.
  • CR (Credits): Earned from visitors. Used for advanced upgrades.
  • CU (Curiosity): Earned from learning facts. Used for educational unlocks.
  • GR (Galactic Reputation): Prestige currency. Earned by Synthesizing moons. Used for permanent Star Cards.

How do I unlock new moons?

After completing Europa, you can unlock additional Jovian moons:

  • Ganymede: 1,000 GR (The Conductor node‚Äîmagnetic auroras!)
  • Callisto: 5,000 GR (The Extractor node‚Äîancient history)
  • Io: 10,000 GR (The Forge node‚Äîvolcanic chaos)

Each moon has unique species, events, and educational content!

‚ôªÔ∏è Prestige (Synthesis Protocol)

What is the Synthesis Protocol?

The Synthesis Protocol is INCEPT≈∏ON's prestige system. When you "Synthesize" a moon, you:

  • Reset your local progress on that moon (resources, species, upgrades)
  • Earn Galactic Reputation (GR) based on total BM + CR accumulated
  • Keep all unlocked Star Cards (permanent multipliers)
  • Keep all Xenogens (permanent species)

GR is calculated using: GR = floor(A * log10(total_BM + total_CR))

When should I Synthesize for the first time?

We recommend Synthesizing Europa when you can earn at least 50-100 GR. This typically takes 30-45 minutes of gameplay. You'll have enough to purchase your first Star Card, which provides a permanent 20% boost!

What are Star Cards?

Star Cards are permanent upgrades purchased with Galactic Reputation. They persist across all Synthesis cycles and apply to all moons. Examples:

  • Observation Amplifier: +20% tap power
  • Evolutionary Catalyst: Species unlock 30% faster
  • Quantum Entanglement: Offline progress +50%

üåô Offline Progress

Does INCEPT≈∏ON work offline?

Yes! INCEPT≈∏ON is completely offline after initial download. Your species continue generating Biomass and Credits while you're away, up to:

  • Base: 8 hours
  • With upgrades: Up to 24 hours

When you return, you'll see a summary of resources earned offline.

Why is my offline progress capped?

To maintain game balance and prevent runaway idle gains. You can extend the cap to 24 hours by purchasing the "Extended Life Support" upgrade in the Engineering tab.

üêõ Troubleshooting

The app is lagging or running slowly

Try these steps:

  1. Enable Reduce Motion in Settings ‚Üí Accessibility (reduces particle effects)
  2. Close other apps running in the background
  3. Restart your device
  4. Ensure you have the latest version of INCEPT≈∏ON from the App Store
  5. Check that your device meets minimum requirements (see below)

INCEPT≈∏ON is optimized for 60 FPS on iPhone 12 and newer. Older devices may experience minor frame drops during particle-heavy events.

My save game is not loading

INCEPT≈∏ON auto-saves every 30 seconds. If your save is corrupted:

  1. Force-close the app (swipe up from app switcher on iOS)
  2. Reopen the app
  3. If the issue persists, email us at support@inceptyon.com with details
Note: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local save data. Back up your progress by not uninstalling if you experience issues.

An event is not triggering

Events trigger every 90-150 seconds (random interval). If an event hasn't triggered:

  • Make sure enough time has passed (wait 2-3 minutes)
  • Check that you're not in a menu (events only trigger in the habitat view)
  • Restart the app

The app crashes on launch

Try these steps:

  1. Restart your device
  2. Ensure iOS is up to date (Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Software Update)
  3. Delete and reinstall INCEPT≈∏ON (‚ö†Ô∏è This will delete your save data)
  4. If the issue persists, email support@inceptyon.com with your device model and iOS version

Audio is not playing

Check the following:

  • Ensure your device is not in Silent Mode (check the physical switch on iPhone)
  • Increase volume using the volume buttons
  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Audio and verify music/SFX are not muted
  • Restart the app
